Group Plans

Offer your employees high quality, affordable dental benefits.

Our group dental plans are designed for the greatest value, with our Quality Assurance guarantee.

Experience

Dental Health Services has provided high quality, affordable dental coverage to California for over 35 years and Washington State for over 25 years and now in Oregon. We believe our commitment to a higher standard of care sets us apart from other dental plans. We pride ourselves on providing professional, personal service to our each of our members.

Flexible Options

As a full service dental benefits company, we provide solutions for all of your oral health needs. We offer a range of dental products including Group Prepaid, PPO, and Indemnity Plans.

Free Proposal

Provide your employees with the benefits they need and they will thank you for it. For your free custom proposal, contact your broker, complete the online Proposal Request Form or call. In California call 800-222-2735, and in Washington and Oregon call 866-585-6464.

Easy & Simple

We invite you to spend some time browsing our website. It is filled with information to make your job easier and will be a resource for both you and your employees.
