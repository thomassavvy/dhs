Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Dental Health Services maintains high standards for the protection of your privacy while visiting our website. Here is what you can expect when you visit www.dentalhealthservices.com:

We won't sell, rent or share any personally-identifiable information you provide We won't send you any unsolicited e-mail ("spam").

When you visit our site, we may place a cookie on your computer that will allow us to customize and enhance your visit. We may also employ third party software to help us gather, sort and manage anonymous data about our visitors. Our cookies will never be used to track your activity on any third party web sites or to send spam, nor will our cookies provide us with any personally identifiable information about you.

Dental Health Services is respectful of the privacy concerns of the visitors to its sites on the Internet. As a general policy, no personal information is automatically collected from visitors to its site. However, certain non-personal visitor information is recorded by the standard operation of Dental Health Services servers. This information is primarily used to provide an enhanced on-line experience for the visitor By having this information, Dental Health Services is able to optimize its web pages automatically for each particular visitor. Other uses of this information include tracking the number of visitors to the sites (but visitors are tracked only in an aggregate and non-personally-identifiable form). E-mail addresses and other personally identifiable data about visitors to this site are known to Dental Health Services only when voluntarily submitted by the visitor. Personally identifiable information that may be collected as a result of a visitor voluntarily providing feedback or subscribing to newsletters is retained by Dental Health Services and not sold or otherwise transferred outside the company. This information is used to better understand visitors' use of our sites and to support transactions made on the sites.
